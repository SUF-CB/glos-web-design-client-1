Sunrise Dolphin Encounter

On the West Coast of Mauritius, we are lucky enough to have wild dolphins that come near the shore to socialize, rest & play every morning. We can encounter two types of dolphins: spinner & bottlenose. We normally see one or the other, except if you are very lucky and meet both species, which happens sometimes!

We love these sea creatures and our goal is to guide our customers through this lifetime experience; passion and respect are our priority. The crew will start the excursion with their briefing and give you details about: security, respect of the dolphins, different spots that you will visit etc…

The departure & arrival is from the L'Estuaire in Black River. We encourage a very early departure for a peaceful and respectful swimming experience with the mammals; 6:15am in Winter season & 5:30am in Summer season. We observe and swim with them for approximately 60min to 90min. Then we cruise to our favorite snorkeling in the Natural Aquarium to see the tropical sea life: clams, fishes, starfish, corals... Last stop of the trip will be the famous Crystal Rock, in the White Lagoon. A fragile coral formation in the middle of crystal clear water.

For your comfort, snorkelling equipment is provided on board as well as soft beverages and homemade banana bread. Please bring along your beach towel

Pssst… Stay calm & positive, the dolphins can feel your energy as they have cerebral & emotional ratios very close to us, humans!

Underwater and Aerial Photography

John and Marine launched this exclusive concept in Mauritius in 2015 as per the increasing demand. We all want the best pictures whether it is for our social media feeds, for the most amazing holiday souvenir or just to share our love for the Ocean!

We will bring you to amazing spots with incredible landscape or scenery. Under water everything is fluid, light and...FUN! Your inspirations are most welcome as we want to make you happy and meet your expectations. Even if you not very comfortable in the water, we can shoot in very shallow water. Also, we will combine underwater shots with aerial photography to complete the session and offer you the best high quality photographs.

We shoot with an Ikelite underwater housing, a Nikon D7500 and DJI Mavic Pro 1 and 2.

We propose two photo shooting packages:

  • - 60min and 2 shooting spots with 20 edited pictures
  • - 120min and 3 shooting spots with 35 edited pictures

Maternity, honeymoon, couple, personal, wedding, family or free diving shooting, let's create and have fun together!

The boat is private, embarking from L'Estuaire, Black River. The pictures will be sent to you via WeTransfer within 6 working days (weekend excluded).

Catamaran Sunset and Dinner Cruise

Hop on board Sea Spirit 40 feet catamaran from La Balise Marina in Black River for a unique 5 hours cruise. The catamaran will sail along the coast line towards Tamarin Bay or Le Morne to watch the most beautiful tropical sunset the island has to offer. With a drink in your hand, good music in the background, you will spend an unforgettable evening at golden hour. Sea Spirit will then return to its mooring in Black River Bay where the crew will start preparing your dinner. A delicious local barbecue will be served.

After dinner, chill on the front deck before cruising back to the pontoon.
N.B: The food & beverages supplement package of Euro 40 per person is compulsory and is to be paid at our office before boarding.
